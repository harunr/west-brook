Introduction

Welcome to Westbrook Estate

Carefully laid out, planned and developed on gentle undulating slopes, Port Elizabeth’s brand new Westbrook estate can cater to your every need.

Westbrook’s nine beautiful residential security estates offer you secure family living in affordable designer homes, top class schooling and even the nation’s foremost retirement lifestyle brand. Positioned close to the city and it's many lifestyle amenities, recreation and leisure facilities.

Far enough away from the busy town, you’ll find that life can be a walk in the park, along with a laidback lifestyle, excellent value for money & sound long term property investment returns. And naturally you’ll relish the freedom to live life to the fullest & enjoy qualities that do not exist elsewhere in Port Elizabeth.
