The Developer

The Amdec Group

The Amdec Group, South Africa’s leading developer of new urban lifestyles.

Amdec believes in a better way of living: in new, connected and sustainable environments - designed and developed for people - where comfort and convenience comes together effortlessly to deliver a thoroughly modern and enjoyable lifestyle. These qualities merge in safe, well-managed estates, along with the freedom and inspiration to dream and to feel entirely at home. With Westbrook, Amdec is creating the first estate of its magnitude and kind in the area

attracting people of all ages and from all walks of life who have been searching for a sanctuary for their families to live, learn, work and play. This forward- thinking development adds to Amdec’s signature property portfolio which includes the iconic Melrose Arch mixed-use precinct in Johannesburg, the Evergreen Lifestyles brand, XtraSpace state-of-the-art self-storage facilities and many other landmark local and international mixed-use, residential and commercial developments.
